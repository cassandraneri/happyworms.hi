ABOUT US

Our goal is to promote the healthy hobbies of vermicomposting and gardening using the perfect worm composting starter kit. Worm composting is the proverbial win-win situation that gives you a convenient way to dispose of unwanted food waste and provides you happy worms which then produce the best organic fertilizer–or as gardeners say, “BLACK GOLD” for your garden. We are pro-mother-earth. We believe in sustainability and giving back to the planet. We approach life with intention and peace.
